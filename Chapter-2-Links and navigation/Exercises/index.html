Plot

Stranger Things is a science fiction horror television series set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana. The plot follows a group of kids who encounter supernatural forces and government experiments when their friend mysteriously disappears.

Key Information

Genre: Science Fiction, Horror, Drama

Created by: The Duffer Brothers

Number of Seasons: 4

Number of Episodes: 25

Original Release: July 15, 2016 - Present

Characters

Stranger Things features a diverse cast of characters, including Eleven, Mike Wheeler, Will Byers, Jim Hopper, Dustin Henderson, Lucas Sinclair, and many others.
